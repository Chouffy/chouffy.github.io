Is a [[Software]] that helps measuring [[network]] speed.

	- Android client: [PingTools](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=ua.com.streamsoft.pingtools)
## Usage
- One server: `iperf3 -s`
- One client: `iperf3 -c <SERVER_IP> -t <DURATION_SECONDS>`
