## Notes
- A 8 Ω [[Speaker]] 
	- Can be used with a 4 Ω [[Audio Amplifier|Amplifier]]
		- Reduce sound quality
		- Risk of sound distortion or speaker blowout
	- Are for low-power output
	- Not suitable for high-frequency
	- Lack clarity
- A 4 Ω [[Speaker]] 
	- Is better for bass
